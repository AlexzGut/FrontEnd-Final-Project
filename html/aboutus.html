A Message from All of Us

Here at "One Less Craft," we wholeheartedly recognize and honor the Traditional Custodians of the lands we inhabit. We hold deep respect for their enduring relationships with the land, sea, and community. We pay homage to their ancestors, both past and present, and extend this profound respect to all Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander communities of today. Just as every thread is woven into a masterpiece, we strive to weave understanding, unity, and reverence into the tapestry of our journey at "One Less Craft."
